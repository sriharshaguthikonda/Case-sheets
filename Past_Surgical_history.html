PAST SURGICAL HISTORY: (Non-gynecological)

  • Any other surgeries, dates, indications, complications:
Surgical History Inquiry "Have you had any operations in the past? What were they for, and roughly when did you have them?"
    • Carotid Endarterectomy
      • Cranial Nerve Injury (e.g., hypoglossal, vagus)
      • Restenosis of the carotid artery
      • Increased short-term stroke risk
    • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
      • Graft Failure / Occlusion
      • Atrial Fibrillation
      • Post-perfusion syndrome ("pump head")
      • Chronic Sternal Pain
    • Varicose Vein Stripping / Ablation
      • Recurrence of varicose veins
      • Skin discoloration (hemosiderin staining)
    • Grommet (Myringotomy Tube) Insertion
      • Persistent Tympanic Membrane Perforation
      • Recurrent otorrhoea (discharge)
      • Tympanosclerosis (scarring of the eardrum)
    • Tonsillectomy
      • Alteration in taste
      • Secondary Post-operative Hemorrhage (often due to infection)
      • Velopharyngeal insufficiency (nasal voice/regurgitation)
    • Thoracotomy / Lung Resection
      • Chronic post-thoracotomy pain
      • Pulmonary complications
      • Reduced lung function
    • Appendectomy
      • Adhesive Small Bowel Obstruction
      • Wound Infection / Abscess formation
      • Incisional Hernia
    • Bariatric Surgery (e.g., Gastric Bypass)
      • Internal Hernia / Bowel Obstruction
      • Dumping Syndrome
      • Nutritional Deficiencies (Iron, B12, Calcium, Vit D)
    • Cholecystectomy (Gallbladder Removal)
      • Bile duct injury (if occurred)
      • Increased risk of bile reflux gastritis
      • Post-cholecystectomy syndrome (dyspepsia, diarrhea)
    • Endoscopy (Gastroscopy or Colonoscopy with biopsy/polypectomy)
      • Ongoing need for surveillance (e.g., for Barrett's esophagus, IBD, colonic polyps)
      • Perforation (rare)
      • Post-polypectomy bleeding
    • Hernia Repair (with mesh)
      • Chronic Post-Herniorrhaphy Pain (Inguinodynia)
      • Mesh Infection or Migration
      • Recurrence of Hernia
    • Large Bowel Resection
      • Change in bowel habits (frequency, consistency)
      • Anastomotic leak
      • Incontinence (if low anterior resection)
    • Small Bowel Resection
      • Anastomotic leak
      • Increased frequency of bowel movements
      • Short gut syndrome / Malabsorption / Vitamin deficiencies
    • Thyroidectomy
      • Lifelong Hypothyroidism (if total removal)
      • Hypoparathyroidism / Hypocalcemia
      • Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve injury / Hoarseness
    • Neurosurgical Procedures
      • Cognitive changes
      • Motor/sensory deficits
      • Seizure disorder
    • Carpal Tunnel Release
      • Pillar pain (pain at the base of the palm)
      • Persistent or recurrent symptoms
      • Scar tenderness / hypersensitivity
    • Spinal Surgery (e.g., Laminectomy, Fusion)
      • Failed Back Surgery Syndrome / Chronic Neuropathic Pain
      • Hardware failure
      • Adjacent Segment Disease
    • Splenectomy
      • Thrombocytosis (high platelet count)
      • Increased lifelong risk from encapsulated bacteria
      • Overwhelming Post-Splenectomy Infection (OPSI)
    • Cataract Surgery
      • Chronic eye inflammation or dryness
      • Retinal Detachment
      • Posterior Capsular Opacification (requiring YAG laser capsulotomy)
    • Skin Lesion / Mole Excision or Biopsy
      • Localised nerve damage / Numbness
      • Hypertrophic or Keloid Scarring
      • Recurrence of lesion
      • Incomplete excision requiring further surgery
    • Total Hip / Knee Replacement
      • Venous Thromboembolism (DVT/PE)
      • Prosthetic Joint Infection
      • Aseptic Loosening
      • Dislocation
      • Periprosthetic Fracture
    • Hysterectomy
      • Premature menopause (if ovaries removed)
      • Pelvic organ prolapse
      • Urinary incontinence
    • LLETZ / LEEP or Cone Biopsy of Cervix
      • Cervical stenosis
      • Changes in menstrual bleeding
      • Increased risk of pre-term labor in future pregnancies
    • Caesarean Section
      • Adhesions / Chronic Pelvic Pain
      • Placenta Accreta/Previa in future pregnancies
      • Uterine Rupture in subsequent labor (VBAC risk)
    • TURP (Transurethral Resection of the Prostate)
      • Recurrence of BPH symptoms
      • Urethral Stricture
      • Retrograde Ejaculation
    • Vasectomy
      • Sperm granuloma
      • Post-vasectomy pain syndrome
    • Prostatectomy
      • Urinary incontinence
      • Erectile dysfunction
