COMPLAINT: SLEEP INSOMNIA / DIFFICULTY SLEEPING

T: Type / Pattern of Insomnia
  • SLEEP ONSET (Difficulty falling asleep)
    • Anxiety / Racing mind
    • Delayed Sleep Phase Syndrome (Circadian)
    • Restless Leg Syndrome
  • SLEEP MAINTENANCE (Waking frequently)
    • Obstructive Sleep Apnea
    • Nocturia (Diabetes/Prostatic)
    • Chronic Pain
    • GERD / Reflux
  • EARLY MORNING AWAKENING
    • Depression
    • Advanced Sleep Phase Syndrome
"Do you have trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or do you wake up too early and can't get back to sleep?"
O: Onset
  • ACUTE (Sudden)
    • Life stress / Grief
    • Acute illness / Fever
    • Jet lag
  • CHRONIC (Gradual)
    • Chronic Anxiety / Depression
    • Poor sleep hygiene habits
    • Undiagnosed Sleep Apnea
"When did the sleep problems start? Was there a specific trigger like a stressful event?"
D: Duration & Frequency
  • TRANSIENT
    • Less than 1 month
  • CHRONIC
    • More than 3 months, >3 nights/week
  • FREQUENCY
    • Every night vs. Intermittent
"How many nights a week does this happen? How long has this been going on?"
C: Causes & Contributors (Aggravating Factors)
  • LIFESTYLE
    • Caffeine / Alcohol intake
    • Late screen time (Blue light)
    • Irregular schedule (Shift work)
    • Daytime napping
  • ENVIRONMENT
    • Noise / Light / Temperature
    • Bed partner disruption
  • MEDICATIONS
    • Steroids
    • Bronchodilators
    • Diuretics (causing nocturia)
    • Antidepressants (SSRIs)
"Do you drink coffee or alcohol near bedtime? Are you on any medications that might keep you awake?"
A: Associated Symptoms (Review of Systems)
  • SNORING / GASPING / CHOKING
    • Obstructive Sleep Apnea
  • LEG SENSATIONS / TWITCHING
    • Restless Leg Syndrome (RLS)
    • Periodic Limb Movement Disorder
  • HEARTBURN / COUGHING
    • Gastroesophageal Reflux (GERD)
  • FREQUENT URINATION (Nocturia)
    • Diabetes Mellitus
    • BPH / Prostate issues
    • Heart Failure
  • PALPITATIONS
    • Anxiety / Panic attacks
    • Hyperthyroidism
    • Arrhythmia
  • PAIN
    • Arthritis / Back pain
    • Neuropathy
  • NIGHT SWEATS
    • Menopause
    • Tuberculosis / Brucellosis
    • Lymphoma
"Do you snore or gasp for air? Do your legs feel restless? Do you have to pee often at night?"
E: Effect on Daytime Function
  • FATIGUE / SLEEPINESS
    • Falling asleep while driving
    • Napping unintentionally
  • COGNITIVE
    • Poor concentration / Memory
    • Irritability / Mood swings
"How do you feel during the day? Have you ever nodded off while driving or working?"
P: Previous Treatments
  • Pharmacological
    • Benzodiazepines
    • Melatonin / Herbal supplements
  • Behavioral
    • CBT-I (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)
    • Sleep Hygiene measures
"Have you tried any sleeping pills or home remedies? Did they help?"
R: Red Flags
  • WITNESSED APNEA / GASPING
    • Severe OSA (Risk of CV event)
  • ACTING OUT DREAMS (Punching/Kicking)
    • REM Behavior Disorder (Parkinson's precursor)
  • SUICIDAL IDEATION
    • Severe Depression
  • SUDDEN ONSET MUSCLE WEAKNESS (Cataplexy)
    • Narcolepsy
  • FALLING ASLEEP DRIVING
    • Public safety risk
"These questions are important to rule out urgent conditions."
S: Severity / Scale
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how much is the lack of sleep affecting your quality of life?"
