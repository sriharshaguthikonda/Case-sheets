• S: Site as in place!
    • Home – meals, bedtime, chores
    • School – class, playground, after-school club
    • Other carers – grandparents, friends’ houses
  • O: Onset
    • date
    • Relation to event
      1. Start school
      2. Life events
    • Sudden
    • vs gradual
  • D: Duration / Frequency
    • Weeks / months
    • Episodes per day
    • Length of each episode
  • A: Aggravating / Relieving
    • Aggravating
      • Fatigue, hunger
      • Transitions, homework, screen-shutdown
      • Unstructured time, loud settings
    • Relieving
      • Calm one-to-one attention
      • Predictable routine, visual schedule
      • Positive reinforcement, time-out corner
  • C: Character / Type
    • Verbal refusal – “no”, shouting
    • Physical – throwing, hitting
    • Withdrawal – ignoring, hiding
  • A: Associated Features
    • Sleep issues, nightmares
    • Inattention, hyperactivity
    • Mood swings, anxiety signs
    • Somatic – tummy-aches, headaches
  • P: Progression
    • Stable, improving, worsening
    • New behaviours emerging
  • P: Pattern
    • Daily timing – mornings, evenings
    • Weekdays vs weekends
    • Term-time vs holidays
  • E: Effect on Life
    • Parental stress, sleep loss
    • School performance, friendships
    • Family activities curtailed
  • R: Red Flags
    • Violence causing injury
    • Self-harm talk or gestures
    • Regression of speech / toileting
    • Safeguarding concerns, bullying
  • S: Severity Scale 0–10
    • Parent-rated distress
    • Teacher-rated impact
