COMPLAINT: INFERTILITY

S: Starting Point & Duration
  • Duration of trying (e.g., > 1 year if female < 35, > 6 months if female > 35)
  • Primary vs. Secondary Infertility (i.e., any previous pregnancies)
"Could you tell me how long you've been trying to conceive? And have you conceived before, with this or any other partner?"
C: Character (Partner-Specific History)
  • FEMALE PARTNER'S HISTORY
    • Menstrual History:
      • Age of menarche, Cycle length & regularity, Duration & volume of flow
      • Dysmenorrhea (painful periods), Intermenstrual or post-coital bleeding
    • Obstetric History:
      • Previous pregnancies & outcomes (live births, miscarriages, terminations, ectopic)
    • Gynaecological History:
      • History of PCOS, Endometriosis, Fibroids, Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
      • Previous abnormal smears / LLETZ procedure
    • Associated Symptoms:
      • Hirsutism, acne, weight changes (PCOS clues)
      • Galactorrhea (nipple discharge) (Hyperprolactinemia clue)
      • Dyspareunia (painful intercourse)
  • MALE PARTNER'S HISTORY
    • Childhood & Medical History:
      • Undescended testes (cryptorchidism)
      • Mumps orchitis
      • Testicular trauma or torsion
    • Surgical History:
      • Hernia repair, Vasectomy/reversal, Testicular surgery
    • Associated Symptoms:
      • Testicular pain, lumps, or swelling
      • Erectile or ejaculatory dysfunction
"To get a full picture, I'll need to ask some detailed questions about both of your medical histories, starting with the female partner."
P: Pattern (Timing & Frequency)
  • Frequency of intercourse per week
  • Timing of intercourse in relation to the menstrual cycle
  • Any pain or specific difficulties during intercourse
"To understand the full picture, I need to ask a few personal questions. How often are you able to have intercourse, and do you have any difficulties?"
A/R: Lifestyle & Environmental Factors
  • Smoking status (both partners)
  • Alcohol consumption (units/week, both partners)
  • Recreational drug use
  • Body Mass Index (BMI) / Diet & Exercise habits
  • Occupation (e.g., exposure to heat, chemicals, radiation)
  • Stress levels
"Sometimes lifestyle can play a part. Could I ask about your diet, exercise, and any habits like smoking, drinking or drug use for both of you?"
R: Previous Investigations & Treatments
  • Female: Day 21 progesterone, other hormonal blood tests (LH, FSH, prolactin), pelvic ultrasound, hysterosalpingogram (HSG)?
  • Male: Semen analysis?
  • Any previous treatments (e.g., Clomiphene, IUI, IVF)?
"Have either of you had any tests or treatments for this before?"
E: Effect on Life (Psychosocial Impact)
  • Stress, anxiety, or feelings of depression
  • Impact on relationship / sexual function
  • Strain on social life / interaction with friends and family
"This can be a very stressful journey. How has this been affecting you both individually and as a couple?"
R: Red Flags (for urgent specialist review)
  • Female age > 35 (or > 40 is very urgent)
  • Amenorrhea (no periods) or very irregular cycles
  • History of chemotherapy or pelvic radiation
  • Known severe endometriosis, PID, or bilateral tubal disease
  • Known severe male factor infertility (e.g., azoospermia)
"I just need to confirm a few details to guide how quickly we need to act."
S: Severity / Distress Scale
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how much distress is this situation causing you?"
