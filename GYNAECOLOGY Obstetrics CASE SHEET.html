      • Transabdominal Ultrasound (standard for obstetric monitoring after 1st trimester)
      • Saline Infusion Sonohysterography (SIS)
      • MRI Pelvis (for complex cases, staging cancer, detailed assessment of fibroids/endometriosis, placenta accreta spectrum)
      • CT Scan (less common for primary Gynae, more for staging/metastasis - generally avoided in pregnancy unless critical)
      • Hysterosalpingography (HSG) - (for infertility assessment)
    • Microbiology/Pathology:
      • High Vaginal Swab (HVS) / Endocervical Swab (for infection - microscopy, culture, NAAT for STIs like Chlamydia/Gonorrhea) (Group B Strep screen in pregnancy).
      • Cervical Smear (Pap test) / HPV testing
      • Endometrial Biopsy (Pipelle, D&C - for AUB, suspected endometrial pathology)
      • Vulval/Vaginal biopsy (if suspicious lesions)
    • Other:
      • Colposcopy (if abnormal cervical screen)
      • Hysteroscopy (diagnostic and operative)
      • Laparoscopy (diagnostic and operative)

  • Treatment Plan:
    • Symptomatic treatment: (e.g., analgesia for pain, anti-emetics if associated nausea)
      • Safe analgesia in pregnancy (Paracetamol first line). Anti-emetics for NVP (e.g., cyclizine, ondansetron).
    • Specific treatment:
      • Obstetric Conditions:
        • Management of Miscarriage (expectant, medical, surgical).
        • Management of Ectopic Pregnancy (expectant, medical - methotrexate, surgical - salpingectomy/salpingostomy).
        • Antenatal Care: Routine schedule, monitoring blood pressure, urine, fetal growth (SFH), fetal wellbeing. Folic acid, Vitamin D. Diet and lifestyle advice.
        • Management of Pre-eclampsia (monitoring, antihypertensives, magnesium sulphate, timing of delivery).
        • Management of GDM (diet, exercise, metformin, insulin).
        • Management of Preterm Labour (tocolytics, antenatal corticosteroids, magnesium sulphate for neuroprotection).
        • Induction of Labour.
        • Intrapartum Care (monitoring, pain relief options, mode of delivery decisions).
        • Management of Postpartum Haemorrhage.
        • Postnatal Care (wound care, breastfeeding support, contraception, mental health screening).
      • Non-Medical (Gynaecological/General):
        • Lifestyle modifications (diet, exercise, weight management for PCOS, fibroids)
        • Pelvic floor muscle training (for SUI, mild prolapse)
        • Psychological support/counseling (for chronic pain, infertility, menopausal symptoms, psychosexual issues)
        • Bladder training (for urge incontinence)
      • Medical (Gynaecological/General):
        • Hormonal therapy (e.g., Combined Oral Contraceptives, Progestogens, Mirena IUS, GnRH agonists - for AUB, endometriosis, fibroids, contraception, menopause)
        • Antibiotics/Antifungals (for PID, vaginitis, UTI)
        • Tranexamic acid, Mefenamic acid (for heavy menstrual bleeding)
        • Iron supplementation (for anemia)
        • Medications for osteoporosis (if postmenopausal and at risk)
        • Pain management for chronic pelvic pain (NSAIDs, neuropathic agents)
      • Surgical (Gynaecological/General):
        • Dilation and Curettage (D&C)
        • Hysteroscopy (polypectomy, myomectomy, endometrial ablation)
        • Laparoscopy (diagnostic, adhesiolysis, ovarian cystectomy, treatment of endometriosis, ectopic pregnancy, tubal surgery)
        • Myomectomy (abdominal, laparoscopic, hysteroscopic)
        • Hysterectomy (abdominal, vaginal, laparoscopic - with/without oophorectomy)
        • Oophorectomy/Salpingo-oophorectomy
        • Pelvic floor repair/Sling procedures (for prolapse, SUI)
        • Cancer staging and debulking surgeries
