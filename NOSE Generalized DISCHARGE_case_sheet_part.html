Discharge

1ST COMPLAINT: DISCHARGE

  • S: Site "Where exactly is the discharge coming from? Your nose, ear, a wound, nipple, or somewhere else?"
    • Nose (Rhinorrhea)
      • Allergic Rhinitis
      • Viral URI (Common Cold) / Sinusitis
      • CSF Rhinorrhea (after head trauma)
    • Ear (Otorrhea)
      • Otitis Externa / Media with perforation
      • Cholesteatoma
    • Vagina
      • Candida (Thrush)
      • Bacterial Vaginosis (BV)
      • STI (e.g., Trichomonas, Chlamydia)
    • Nipple
      • Duct Ectasia / Intraductal Papilloma
      • Galactorrhea (prolactin issue)
      • Malignancy
    • Urethra
      • Urethritis (STI)
    • Wound / Skin
  • O: Onset "When did the discharge start? Was it sudden or gradual?"
  • D: Duration "How long has this been happening?"
  • A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors "Does anything make it better or worse? For example, any medications or specific times of day?"
  • C: Character "Can you describe the discharge? What color is it, how thick is it, and is there any odor?"
    • COLOR
      • Clear / Watery: Allergic, Viral, or CSF leak
      • White: Candida, physiological
      • Yellow / Green (Purulent): Bacterial infection
      • Bloody (Sanguineous): Trauma, Infection, Malignancy
    • CONSISTENCY
      • Thin / Watery
      • Thick / Mucoid / Purulent
      • Cheesy / Curd-like (classic for Candida)
    • ODOR
      • Foul-smelling (suggests bacterial infection, e.g., anaerobic)
      • Fishy (classic for Bacterial Vaginosis)
    • AMOUNT (e.g., spotting, soaking through clothes/dressings)
  • R: Radiation (of associated pain) "Is there any pain associated with the discharge, and does it travel anywhere?"
  • A: Associated Symptoms "Have you noticed any other symptoms along with the discharge?"
    • NASAL / SINUS
      • Itching, sneezing, watery eyes (suggests allergy)
      • Facial pain/pressure, fever, cough (suggests sinusitis)
      • Sore throat, malaise (suggests viral URI)
    • EAR
      • Ear pain (otalgia), hearing loss, dizziness (vertigo)
    • VAGINAL / URETHRAL
      • Itching (pruritus), soreness, painful intercourse (dyspareunia)
      • Abnormal bleeding, lower abdominal pain
    • NIPPLE
      • Breast lump, skin changes (peau d'orange), nipple inversion
    • SYSTEMIC
      • Fever, chills, feeling generally unwell
  • P: Progression "Has the nature of the discharge changed since it started (e.g., in color or amount)?"
  • P: Pattern "Is there a pattern to it? Is it constant, or does it come and go?"
    • e.g., Nasal discharge worse on bending forward (CSF leak)
    • e.g., Vaginal discharge related to menstrual cycle
  • E: Effect on Life "How has this discharge been affecting you? Is it causing discomfort or embarrassment?"
    • Social embarrassment
    • Discomfort, inconvenience (e.g., soiling clothes)
  • R: Red Flags "I just need to ask some specific questions to make sure we're not missing anything serious."
    • CLEAR, WATERY NASAL DISCHARGE AFTER HEAD TRAUMA
      • CSF Rhinorrhea
    • UNILATERAL, SPONTANEOUS, BLOODY NIPPLE DISCHARGE
      • Possible breast cancer
    • FOUL-SMELLING DISCHARGE WITH FEVER, TACHYCARDIA, or CONFUSION
      • Signs of Sepsis
    • POST-MENOPAUSAL VAGINAL BLEEDING or bloody discharge
      • Endometrial cancer until proven otherwise
  • S: Severity / Scale "On a scale of 1 to 10, how much is this discharge bothering you?"
