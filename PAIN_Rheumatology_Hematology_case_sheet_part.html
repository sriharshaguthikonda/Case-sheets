1ST COMPLAINT: PAIN

  • S: Site "Can you show me exactly where the pain is? Is it in one spot or all over?"
    • Localized
    • Widespread/Diffuse?

    • Muscle,
    • bone, or
    • along a nerve ?
    • Specific joints ?
    • (Small or
    • Large?
    • Symmetrical?)
  • O: Onset "When did the pain start? Was there any specific trigger, like an injury or illness?"
    • Sudden or
    •  Gradual?

    • Associated with a
    • specific event,
    • injury, or
    • new medication?
  • D: Duration "How long does the pain last when you experience it? Minutes, hours, or all day?"
  • A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors "What makes the pain better or worse?"
    • MAKES IT WORSE: 
    • Movement,
    • specific activities,
    • posture? (Worse with activity suggests mechanical; worse with rest suggests inflammatory)

    • MAKES IT BETTER: 
    • Rest,
    • activity,
    • specific positions,
    • heat/cold?
  • C: Character "Can you describe the pain for me? Is it aching, sharp, burning, or throbbing?"
    • Aching, Sharp, Burning, Throbbing, Dull?
    • Constant or Intermittent?
    • Is there any stiffness associated with it, especially in the morning?
  • R: Radiation "Does the pain seem to travel or spread anywhere else?"
  • A: Associated Symptoms "Along with the pain, have you experienced any other symptoms?"
    • SYSTEMIC SYMPTOMS
      • Fever, Chills, Night sweats
      • Fatigue, Malaise
      • Weight loss, Appetite change
    • RHEUMATOLOGY SPECIFICS
      • Joints: Swelling, Redness, Warmth, Morning stiffness (how long does it last?), decreased range of motion.
      • Skin: Rash (type?), Nodules, Photosensitivity.
      • Sicca Symptoms: Dry eyes, dry mouth.
      • Neurological: Tingling, numbness, Raynaud's phenomenon.
      • Other: Muscle weakness, enthesitis (e.g., heel pain), inflammatory back pain, eye inflammation.
    • HEMATOLOGY SPECIFICS
      • Bleeding: Easy bruising, Petechiae, Epistaxis, Gum bleeding, Menorrhagia, Hematuria, GI bleed.
      • Thrombosis: Limb swelling/pain (DVT?), Shortness of breath/Chest pain (PE?).
      • Anemia: Pallor, Dyspnea on exertion, Dizziness.
      • Other: Lymphadenopathy, Hepatosplenomegaly, Bone pain, Recurrent infections, Pruritus (itching).
  • P: Progression "Has the pain changed since it first started? Is it getting worse?"
  • P: Pattern / Timing /  "Is there a pattern to the pain? What treatments have you tried?"
    • TIMING:
    • morning/night?
    • With activity/rest?
    • Does it wake you from sleep?
    • Pattern of flare-ups?
    • TRAUMA:
    • history ?
  • E: Effect on Life  "How has this pain impacted your daily life, work, sleep, and mood?"
    • Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
    • Work / Hobbies / Social life
    • Sleep
    • Mood
  • R: Red Flags "I must ask some specific questions to rule out anything serious."
    • SYSTEMIC:
      • Unexplained fever,
      • night sweats,
      • weight loss.
    • NEUROVASCULAR:
      • Tingling,
      • Numbness,
      • compartment syndrome?!
    • RHEUMATOLOGY:
      • Acute hot, swollen joint (septic arthritis?)
      • Rapidly progressive neurological deficit
      • Symptoms of GCA (temporal pain, visual changes, jaw claudication)

    • HEMATOLOGY:
      • Symptoms of acute leukemia (severe fatigue, bleeding, infection)
      • Spinal cord compression
      • Severe unexplained bleeding or thrombosis
  • S: Severity / Scale "On a scale of 0 to 10, could you rate your pain: currently, at its best, at its worst, and on average?"
