  • AGE "Thanks Margaret,.....how can i help you?...i see that you are here for your diabetes review.?"
    • < 20
      • Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
      • PCOS
      • Anemia
      • Teenage Pregnancy (Higher risk of pre‑eclampsia, preterm birth, low birth weight)
    • 20 – 29
      • Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID)
      • Menstrual Disorders
      • PCOS
      • Ectopic Pregnancy
      • Infertility
    • 30 – 39
      • Menstrual Disorders
      • PCOS
      • Ectopic Pregnancy
      • Infertility (declining fertility)
      • Mastitis
      • Higher risk of chromosomal abnormalities, GDM, PIH with advancing age
    • 40 – 49
      • Perimenopause
      • Menopause
      • Ectopic Pregnancy
      • Infertility (significantly reduced fertility)
      • Mastitis
      • Advanced Maternal Age: Increased risk of GDM, PIH, chromosomal abnormalities, C‑section
    • 50 – 59
      • Menopause
    • > 60
      • Vaginal Cancer
      • Breast Cancer
    • >>65
      • AAAS
