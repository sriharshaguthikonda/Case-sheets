2ND COMPLAINT: PELVIC PAIN

S: Site
  • RLQ / LLQ
  • Suprapubic
  • Unilateral / Bilateral
  • Deep vs Superficial
"Where exactly is the pain? Right/Left lower abdomen, suprapubic, deep inside pelvis, or superficial?"
O: Onset
  • Sudden: Ovarian torsion, Ruptured ectopic, PID flare
  • Gradual: Endometriosis, Chronic PID, Fibroids
"When did it start? Was it sudden or gradual? Any trigger?"
D: Duration
  • Acute (< 3 months)
  • Chronic (> 6 months)
"How long has this been going on?"
A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors
  • Aggravated by: Menstruation, Ovulation (mittelschmerz), Intercourse, Urination, Defecation, Movement, Specific foods
  • Relieved by: Rest, Position change, Heat, Analgesics, Bowel movement
"What makes it worse or better?"
C: Character
  • Sharp / Stabbing
  • Dull / Aching
  • Cramping
  • Burning / Throbbing
"What does it feel like?"
R: Radiation
  • Back
  • Thighs
  • Perineum
  • Shoulder tip (Kehr's sign)
"Does it travel anywhere?"
A: Associated Symptoms
  • Fever, Chills
  • Vaginal discharge (color, odor, consistency)
  • Nausea, Vomiting
  • Bowel: Diarrhea, Constipation, Bloating, Rectal pain
  • Urinary: Dysuria, Frequency, Urgency, Hematuria
  • Dyspareunia
  • Symptoms of pregnancy
  • Syncope, Dizziness
"Any of these with the pain?"
P: Pattern (Timing/Frequency)
  • Continuous
  • Intermittent
  • Relation to menstrual cycle (cyclic)
"Is it continuous or intermittent? Any relation to periods?"
P: Progression
"Is it getting worse, better, or the same?"
E: Effect on Life
"How does this affect your daily activities, work, sexual function, or mood?"
R: Red Flags
  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Dizziness / Fainting
  • Shoulder tip pain
  • Loin pain
  • Fever / Chills / Rigors
  • Bloody urine
  • Bloody stools
"I need to ask a few questions to rule out serious causes."
S: Severity / Scale
"On a scale of 0 to 10, how severe is the pain?"
