ELABORATION OF ILLICIT DRUG USAGE

1. Character of Use
  • SUBSTANCE(S) USED
    • "Primary" drug (main drug of choice)
    • Other substances used (e.g., cannabis, cocaine, heroin, benzodiazepines, amphetamines, NPS)
  • ROUTE OF ADMINISTRATION
    • Smoked, Snorted (intranasal), Injected (IV/IM), Swallowed (oral)?
"To start, could you tell me which substances you use? It's helpful to go through them one by one."
2. Pattern & Progression
  • ONSET & PROGRESSION
    • Age of first use
    • Has the amount or frequency increased over time? (Tolerance)
    • Any periods of abstinence in the past?
  • QUANTITY & FREQUENCY
    • How much is used on a typical day? (e.g., bags, grams, rocks)
    • How much money is spent per day/week?
    • Last time used?
"Thinking about your main substance, could you walk me through a typical week of use? When did you first start using, and has that changed over time?"
3. Features of Dependence
  • Tolerance: Needing more for the same effect?
  • Withdrawal: Experiencing physical or psychological symptoms on cessation? (e.g., sweats, shakes, anxiety, cramps)
  • Compulsion: A strong desire or craving to use?
  • Loss of Control: Using more or for longer than intended?
  • Salience: Spending a great deal of time obtaining, using, or recovering from the drug?
  • Use Despite Harm: Continued use despite knowing it's causing problems?
"Have you noticed that you need to use more to get the same effect? And what happens if you try to stop or cut down?"
4. Effect on Life (Harm Assessment)
  • PHYSICAL HEALTH
    • Blood-borne viruses (Hepatitis B/C, HIV - from injecting)
    • Skin/soft tissue infections (abscesses, cellulitis)
    • Cardiovascular complications (e.g., endocarditis, palpitations)
    • Respiratory issues (from smoking)
  • MENTAL HEALTH
    • Associated anxiety, depression, paranoia, psychosis?
    • Impact on memory or concentration?
  • SOCIAL FUNCTIONING
    • Impact on relationships with family and friends?
    • Impact on work, education, or housing?
    • Any involvement with the criminal justice system?
"How has your drug use affected different areas of your life?"
5. Risk Behaviours
  • Injecting practices (sharing needles, spoons, water)
  • History of overdose? (What happened, was naloxone used?)
  • Risky situations to fund use? (e.g., sex work, crime)
"I need to ask some specific safety questions. Do you ever inject or share any equipment? And have you ever had an overdose?"
6. Insight, Concerns & Expectations
  • Insight: Does the person see their use as a problem?
  • Motivation:
    • On a scale of 1-10, how important is it for you to make a change?
    • On a scale of 1-10, how confident do you feel that you could make a change?
  • Expectations: What are you hoping to get from this appointment today? Have you tried to get help before?
"What are your thoughts about your current situation? What are the good things and the not-so-good things about your use for you?"
