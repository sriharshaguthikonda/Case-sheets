    3. For each job, ask about
      • Duties:
      • Environment:
      • Protective Equipment:

    • Construction Workers
      • Asbestos exposure (Mesothelioma, Asbestosis, Lung Cancer)
      • Silica dust (Silicosis, Lung Cancer, increased autoimmune risk)
      • Cement dermatitis / Irritant contact dermatitis
      • Noise-induced hearing loss
      • Vibration exposure (Hand-Arm Vibration Syndrome - HAVS)
    • Factory/Manufacturing Workers
      • Noise-induced hearing loss
      • Occupational Asthma (from isocyanates, chemicals)
      • Byssinosis (cotton dust), Siderosis (iron fumes)
      • Vibration exposure (HAVS)
    • Musicians and Audio Engineers
      • Noise-induced hearing loss
      • Repetitive strain injuries
    • Airport Ground Staff
      • Aircraft engine noise
      • Jet fuel exposure
    • Military Personnel
      • Firearms, explosions, and heavy machinery noise
      • PTSD, Stress-related disorders
      • Burn pit exposure
    • Farmers and Agricultural Workers
      • Farmer's Lung / Hypersensitivity Pneumonitis (from moldy hay/grain)
      • Occupational Asthma (pollen, grain dust)
      • Zoonotic infections (e.g., Q fever, Leptospirosis, Brucellosis)
      • Organophosphate / Pesticide exposure
      • Chronic sun exposure (Skin Cancers)
    • Miners
      • Coal dust (Coal Worker's Pneumoconiosis - CWP)
      • Silica dust (Silicosis)
      • Asbestos exposure
      • Noise-induced hearing loss
    • Carpenters/Woodworkers
      • Power tools and machinery noise
      • Wood dust exposure
      • Contact dermatitis from wood treatments
    • Emergency Services
      • Sirens and emergency equipment noise
      • Stress, Burnout, PTSD
      • Smoke and chemical exposure
    • Entertainment Industry
      • Loud music and event noise
      • Irregular hours, stress
    • Transportation Workers
      • Truck, bus, or train engine noise
      • Sedentary work risks
      • Risk of hypoglycemia (for diabetic drivers)
    • Dentists/Dental Technicians
      • High-speed drills noise
      • Neck and back strain
      • Latex allergy
    • Healthcare Workers
      • Blood-borne virus exposure (HIV, Hepatitis B/C)
      • Tuberculosis (TB) exposure
      • Latex allergy / Contact dermatitis from chemicals
      • Back strain from manual handling
      • Stress, Burnout, PTSD
    • Hairdressers, Beauticians
      • Irritant & Allergic Contact Dermatitis (from chemicals)
      • Hair spray and chemical inhalation
      • Varicose veins from prolonged standing
    • Cleaners, Catering, Food Processing
      • Irritant & Allergic Contact Dermatitis (from chemicals, water)
      • Occupational Asthma (e.g., from flour - "Baker's Asthma")
      • Repetitive strain injuries
    • Office Workers
      • Repetitive Strain Injury (RSI), Carpal Tunnel Syndrome
      • Low back pain / Neck pain from posture
      • Increased cardiovascular and metabolic risk
      • Eye strain
    • Shift Workers
      • Sleep disruption, Stress, Burnout
      • Increased risk of metabolic syndrome, cardiovascular disease, and GI issues
