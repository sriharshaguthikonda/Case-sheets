1ST COMPLAINT: CONSTIPATION

  • S: Site of Discomfort "Can you describe the discomfort? Is it a general abdominal bloating, or a feeling of blockage lower down?"
    • GENERALISED BLOATING/FULLNESS
      • Slow-transit constipation
      • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS-C)
    • RECTAL FULLNESS / PRESSURE / BLOCKAGE
      • Obstructive defecation
      • Pelvic floor dyssynergia
      • Rectocele
    • LOWER ABDOMINAL CRAMPING PAIN
      • IBS-C
      • Diverticular disease
  • O: Onset "When did this constipation begin? Has it been lifelong, or is this a recent change?"
    • ACUTE / RECENT (Days to weeks)
      • Bowel obstruction
      • Medication-induced (e.g., opioids, anticholinergics)
      • Change in diet/activity, dehydration
    • CHRONIC (Months to years)
      • Functional constipation / IBS-C
      • Hypothyroidism
      • Neurological disorders (Parkinson's, MS)
  • D: Duration & Frequency "On average, how many days do you go between bowel movements?"
  • A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors "What seems to make it worse? What have you tried that helps?"
    • AGGRAVATING
      • Low fiber , low fluid , inactivity
      • Stress
      • Certain foods (e.g., dairy, processed foods)
      • Medication (e.g., opioids, anticholinergics)
      • Pain in back passage
    • RELIEVING
      • Exercise, increased fluids, fiber
      • Laxatives (ask which type and frequency)
      • Specific foods (prunes, coffee)
  • C: Character "What do your stools typically look like? Are they hard, small pellets? Do you have to strain excessively?"
    • STOOL FORM (Bristol Stool Chart)
      • Type 1 (separate hard lumps)
      • Type 2 (lumpy, sausage-shaped)
    • STRAINING / DIFFICULTY
      • Pelvic floor dyssynergia
      • Obstructive defecation
    • SENSE OF INCOMPLETE EVACUATION
      • Functional constipation
      • Rectal prolapse
  • R: Radiation "Do you get any pain that seems to spread from your abdomen?"
  • A: Associated Symptoms "Along with the constipation, have you experienced anything else like pain, blood, or weight changes?"
    • ABDOMINAL PAIN/BLOATING
      • IBS-C
    • NAUSEA / VOMITING
      • Bowel obstruction
    • BLOOD IN STOOL (Hematochezia)
      • Colorectal cancer
      • Diverticulosis, hemorrhoids, fissures
    • UNEXPLAINED WEIGHT LOSS
      • Malignancy
      • Hyperthyroidism (less common)
    • FATIGUE, COLD INTOLERANCE, DRY SKIN
      • Hypothyroidism
    • NEED FOR MANUAL MANEUVERS (e.g., splinting)
      • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • P: Progression "Over the last few months, would you say the constipation has been getting worse?"
    • PROGRESSIVELY WORSENING
      • Suggests an organic cause, e.g., growing obstruction/tumor
  • P: Pattern "Is it a constant problem, or does it alternate with periods of normal bowel movements or diarrhea?"
    • ALTERNATING WITH DIARRHEA
      • IBS-M (Mixed type)
    • CHRONIC & PERSISTENT
      • Functional / Slow-transit constipation
  • E: Effect on Life "How has this been impacting your daily life, comfort, and well-being?"
  • R: Red Flags "I just need to ask some specific questions to make sure we're not missing anything serious."
    • NEW ONSET > 50 YEARS
      • Increased risk of colorectal cancer
    • UNEXPLAINED WEIGHT LOSS
      • BLOOD IN STOOL
        • Cancer, IBD, significant bleed
      • SEVERE, PERSISTENT PAIN or VOMITING
        • Bowel obstruction
      • IRON DEFICIENCY ANEMIA
        • Suggests chronic GI blood loss
    • S: Severity / Bother Scale "On a scale of 1 to 10, how much does this constipation bother you?"
      • what is baseline?
      • just stools
      • no gas
      • hard stools
