COMPLAINT: TIREDNESS / FATIGUE

  • S: Site (Location of skin lesion/rash)
  • S: Severity & Sleep
    • On a scale of 0-10, how severe is your tiredness?
    • Describe your sleep: How many hours? Is it restorative? Do you have trouble falling/staying asleep? Snoring?
  • O: Onset
    • When did it start? Was it sudden or gradual?
    • Was it linked to an illness, event, or new medication?
  • D: Duration
    • How long has this been going on (weeks, months, years)?
  • A/R: Aggravating / Relieving Factors
    • Worse with: Physical exertion? Mental effort? Stress?
    • Better with: Does rest or sleep actually improve it?
  • C: Character / Type
    • Is it more 
    • physical ("heavy," weak) or 
    • mental ("brain fog," poor concentration)?
  • R: Radiation
    • Not applicable.

Systems Review / DD Checklist:

  • Psychiatric: Depression, Anxiety
  • Endocrine: Hypothyroidism, Diabetes, Adrenal insufficiency
  • Haematology: Anemia, Malignancy
  • Cardio-Respiratory: Heart failure, COPD
  • Infectious: Chronic infection (e.g., EBV), Post-viral fatigue
  • Oncology: Any
  • Genito-Urinary
  • Neuromuscular
  • Musculoskeletal: Fibromyalgia, Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (ME/CFS)
  • Gastrointestinal: Malabsorption (e.g., Celiac disease)
