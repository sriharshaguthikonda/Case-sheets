• S: Site
    • Where exactly is the pain located? (e.g., one side of the head, both sides, forehead, back of the head, behind the eye)
    • Is the location always the same with each headache?
  • O: Onset
    • When did this specific headache start? When did you start getting these types of headaches?
    • Was the onset sudden and severe (like a "thunderclap") or did it build up gradually?
    • What were you doing when it started?
  • D: Duration
    • How long do the headaches typically last if untreated? (minutes, hours, days)
    • How long has this current episode been going on?
  • A/R: Aggravating / Relieving Factors
    • Aggravating Factors: What makes the headache worse? (e.g.,
      • lights (photophobia),
      • noise (phonophobia),
      • Positions
      • movement,
      •  coughing,
      • specific foods,
      • stress, lack of sleep)
    • Relieving Factors: What, if anything, makes it better? (e.g.,
      • lying down
      • dark,
      • quiet room,
      • sleep,
      • medications,
      • applying pressure)
  • C: Character / Type
    • What does the pain feel like? (e.g., throbbing, pulsing, stabbing, sharp, dull, squeezing, pressure-like, burning)
  • R: Radiation
    • Does the pain travel or radiate to any other areas? (e.g., to the neck, shoulders, jaw, face, or teeth)
  • A: Associated Symptoms
    • Nausea or vomiting?
    • Visual changes (aura)? (e.g., flashing lights, zig-zag lines, blind spots)
    • Numbness, tingling, or weakness in any part of the body?
    • Dizziness or vertigo?
    • Stiff neck, fever, chills?
    • Watery or red eye, runny or stuffy nose on one side?
    • Difficulty speaking or confusion?
  • P: Progression
    • How has the pattern of your headaches changed over time? Are they becoming more frequent or more severe?
    • Is this headache different from your usual headaches?
    • (Especially for post-treatment: How has the headache changed since starting therapy?)
  • P: Pattern
    • Continuous or Intermittent: Is the pain constant or does it come and go?
    • Frequency: How often do you get these headaches? (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly)
    • Is there a pattern? (e.g., related to your menstrual cycle, weekends, time of day)
  • E: Effect on Life
    • How does the headache affect your daily life? (e.g., ability to work, attend school, sleep, or socialize)
    • Do you have to stop your activities and rest when you have one?
  • R: Red Flags
    • SAH
    • Is this the "worst headache of your life"?
    • Did it start very suddenly ("thunderclap" headache)?
    • Is it associated with fever and a stiff neck?
    • .
    • Giant cell PMR relation
    • hip and shoulde pain?
    • confusion, weakness, seizure, or personality change?
    • > age of 50?
    • Does it get worse with coughing or straining?
    • Is it getting progressively worse over days or weeks?
  • S: Scale of Zero to 10
    • On a scale from 0 (no pain) to 10 (the worst pain imaginable), how would you rate this headache at its peak?
    • What is the pain level right now?
