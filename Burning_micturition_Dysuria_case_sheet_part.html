1ST COMPLAINT: DYSURIA (Painful Urination)

S: Site of Pain
  • URETHRAL / INTERNAL:
    • UTI, Urethritis
  • EXTERNAL (Vulvar/Labial burning):
    • Vaginitis, Herpes, Dermatitis
  • SUPRAPUBIC / PELVIC:
    • Cystitis
    • Interstitial Cystitis
    • Bladder Cancer
  • FLANK / BACK PAIN:
    • Pyelonephritis
    • Kidney Stone
    • Radiation-induced cystitis
  • PERINEAL / GENITAL AREA:
    • Prostatitis
    • Epididymo-orchitis
    • Urethral trauma
"Where exactly do you feel the pain? Is it inside, on the outside, in your abdomen, or your back?"
O: Onset
  1. start of urination
  2. middle
  3. end
"When did this begin? Was it sudden or has it been a chronic issue?"
D: Duration
  • ACUTE (Hours-Days):
    • UTI, STI
  • CHRONIC / RECURRENT (Weeks-Months):
    • Interstitial Cystitis
    • Chronic Prostatitis

"How long does the pain last when it happens?"
A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors
"What makes it worse or better?"
C: Character/ Characterize the Urine<
  • BURNING:
    • UTI / Urethritis
    • Medication-induced (cyclophosphamide, allopurinol, NSAIDs)
    • Chemical irritants (soaps, spermicides, vaginal lubricants)
    • Foreign body (catheter, bladder stone)
  • SHARP / STABBING:
    • Kidney Stone
  • PRESSURE:
    • Interstitial Cystitis
    • Prostatitis
  • URINE
    • odour
    • colour
    • cloudiness / clarity
    • sediment
"What does the pain feel like? A burn, a sharp pain, or pressure?"
R: Radiation
  • LOIN TO GROIN / TESTICLES:
    • Kidney Stone
  • TO PERINEUM / TIP OF PENIS:
    • Prostatitis
"Does the pain travel anywhere?"
A: Associated Symptoms
  • CLUES FOR UTI
    • Frequency, Urgency, Nocturia
    • Hesitancy
    • Hematuria (blood in urine)
    • Cloudy or foul-smelling urine
  • CLUES FOR PROSTATIC ISSUES (BPH / Prostatitis)
    • Frequency, Urgency, Nocturia
    • Hesitancy, Weak Stream
    • Incontinence, Post-void Dribbling
    • Feeling of incomplete emptying
    • Testicular or Perineal Pain
  • CLUES FOR STI / VAGINITIS
    • Urethral or Vaginal Discharge
    • Genital sores, ulcers, or lesions (Herpes)
    • Vaginal itching or odor (Vaginitis)
    • (See Sexual History for detailed inquiry)
  • AUTOIMMUNE/RHEUMATOLOGICAL CAUSES
    • Reactive arthritis (Reiter syndrome)
    • Behçet's disease
    • Sjögren's syndrome
    • Rheumatoid arthritis (rare)
  • PARASITIC INFECTIONS
    • Urinary schistosomiasis
  • ABDOMINAL EMERGENCIES
    • Appendicitis (irritation of urinary structures)
    • Ectopic pregnancy (irritation of urinary structures)
  • SYSTEMIC SYMPTOMS
    • Fever, Chills, Rigors, Nausea, Vomiting -> Pyelonephritis, Acute Prostatitis
"Along with the pain, have you had a fever, changes in your urine, or any discharge?"
P: Progression
"Since it started, has it gotten worse?"
P: Pattern
  • TIMING OF PAIN
    • Initial (at the start): Suggests Urethritis
    • Terminal (at the end): Suggests Cystitis
  • FREQUENCY
    • Continuous vs. Intermittent
"Is the pain constant or intermittent? When exactly during urination does it hurt?"
E: Effect on Life
"How is this affecting your daily life?"
R: Red Flags
  • PYELONEPHRITIS
    • Fever, chills, rigors, flank pain, vomiting
  • SUSPICION OF MALIGNANCY (Prostate/Bladder)
    • Gross Hematuria (visible blood)
    • Unexplained weight loss, tiredness
    • New onset bone pain (especially back pain)
    • Palpable lumps/nodes
  • ACUTE URINARY RETENTION (unable to pass urine)
  • IMMUNOCOMPROMISED PATIENT or PREGNANCY
"I just need to ask some specific questions to rule out anything serious."
S: Severity / Scale
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate the pain?"
