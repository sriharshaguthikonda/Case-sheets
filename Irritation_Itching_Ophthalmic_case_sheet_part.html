1ST COMPLAINT: EYE IRRITATION / ITCHING

S: Site & Laterality
  • UNILATERAL (One Eye)
    • Foreign body,
    • Blocked tear duct,
    • Herpes Zoster
    • Episcleritis
  • BILATERAL (Both Eyes)
    • Allergic or Viral Conjunctivitis
    • Dry Eye Disease, Blepharitis
"Which eye is bothering you? Is it one or both? Is the feeling on the eyeball itself, or more on the eyelids?"
O: Onset
  • SUDDEN: Allergy, Foreign Body, Chemical Splash, Subconjunctival Hemorrhage (after strain/trauma)
  • GRADUAL: Dry Eye, Blepharitis, Viral/Bacterial Conjunctivitis
"When did you first notice this? Did it come on suddenly or gradually over time?"
D: Duration
"How long has this been going on? Is it constant or does it come and go?"
A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors
  • AGGRAVATING:
  • Contact lenses
  • eye drops
  • Screen use/reading, wind, A/C (Dry Eye);
  • Bright light (Photophobia);
  • Known allergens (pollen, pets).
  • exposure to chemicals
  • trauma
  • swimming
  • recent cosmotics
  • RELIEVING:
  • Artificial tears/lubricating drops;
  • Antihistamine drops;
  • Cool compresses;
  • Resting eyes.
"What makes it feel worse? Screens, wind, bright lights? What helps? Drops, rest?"
C: Character of Discomfort
  • ITCHING (Pruritus): Hallmark of Allergic Conjunctivitis.
  • GRITTY / SANDY / FOREIGN BODY SENSATION: Classic for Dry Eye or Viral Conjunctivitis.
  • BURNING: Common in Dry Eye and Blepharitis (eyelid inflammation).
  • SORENESS / DULL ACHE: Episcleritis, Scleritis.
  • STICKINESS / MATTING OF LIDS (esp. on waking): Classic for Bacterial Conjunctivitis.
"Can you describe the feeling? Is it mainly itchy, gritty, burning, or more of a soreness?"
R: Radiation
"Is there any pain that spreads from the eye, for example, a headache?"
A: Associated Symptoms
  • DISCHARGE
    • Watery / Tearing (Epiphora): Viral, Allergic, Dry Eye (reflex tearing)
    • Stringy, white mucoid: Allergic
    • Purulent (pus, yellow/green): Bacterial
  • VISUAL CHANGES
    • Blurred vision that clears with blinking? (Suggests tear film/discharge issue)
    • Constant blurred vision? Halos around lights? (Red Flags)
  • APPEARANCE
    • Redness (diffuse or in one sector?), Lid swelling, Crusting on lashes
  • SYSTEMIC SYMPTOMS
    • Fever, cough, runny nose? (Viral prodrome)
    • Sneezing, nasal congestion? (Allergies)
    • Joint pain, dry mouth? (Autoimmune, e.g., Sjogren's)
    • Nausea / Vomiting? (Glaucoma red flag)
  • Shingles
"Besides the irritation, have you noticed any discharge, redness, or changes in your vision?"
P: Progression
  • Very Rapid = Inflammatory
  • Slowly = Benign process (e.g., pterygium)
  • Static -> Rapid = Suggests change in a chronic condition
"Since it started, has the issue gotten better, worse, or stayed about the same?"
P: Pattern
  • Worse in the morning (lids matted shut): Bacterial Conjunctivitis
  • Worse as the day progresses (after screen use/reading): Dry Eye Disease
"Is it worse at a particular time of day, like in the morning or at the end of the day?"
E: Effect on Life
"How is this affecting your daily activities like reading, driving, or work? Does it interfere with movement?"
R: Red Flags
  • SEVERE PAIN
  • SIGNIFICANT VISION LOSS (not cleared by blinking)
  • PHOTOPHOBIA (severe light sensitivity)
  • HALOS AROUND LIGHTS, NAUSEA/VOMITING (Acute Glaucoma)
  • CHEMICAL SPLASH / PENETRATING TRAUMA
  • ABNORMAL PUPIL SHAPE OR SIZE
"I must ask some specific questions to make sure we're not missing anything more serious."
S: Severity / Bother Scale
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how bothersome is this irritation?"
