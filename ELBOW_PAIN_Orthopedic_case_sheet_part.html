1ST COMPLAINT: ELBOW PAIN

  • S: Site "Where exactly is the pain in your elbow? Can you point with one finger?"
    • LATERAL (Outside)
      • Tennis Elbow
      • Radial tunnel syndrome
    • MEDIAL (Inside)
      • Golfer's Elbow
      • Cubital Tunnel Syndrome
    • POSTERIOR (Back)
      • Olecranon bursitis
      • Triceps tendinopathy
    • ANTERIOR (Front)
      • Distal biceps tendinopathy
  • O: Onset "When did the pain start? Was it from an injury, or did it come on gradually?"
    • ACUTE / SUDDEN
      • Fracture/Dislocation
      • Tendon/Ligament rupture
    • GRADUAL / CHRONIC
      • Overuse (Epicondylitis)
      • Arthritis
  • D: Duration "How long have you had this pain for? Days, weeks, or months?"
  • A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors "What makes the pain worse? What makes it better?"
    • AGGRAVATING
      • Gripping, lifting, twisting forearm (Epicondylitis)
      • Pressure on the area
    • RELIEVING
      • Rest
      • Ice/Heat
      • Avoiding certain movements
  • C: Character "What does the pain feel like? Is it a sharp pain, a dull ache, burning, or tingling?"
    • DULL ACHE
      • Epicondylitis
      • Osteoarthritis
    • BURNING / TINGLING
      • Nerve entrapment
      • Cervical radiculopathy
    • SHARP / STABBING
      • Loose body
      • Acute tear
    • THROBBING
      • Infection
      • Severe bursitis
  • R: Radiation "Does the pain travel anywhere from your elbow?"
    • DOWN FOREARM (Lateral)
      • Tennis Elbow
    • TO 4TH/5TH FINGERS (Medial)
      • Cubital Tunnel Syndrome
    • FROM NECK/SHOULDER
      • Cervical Radiculopathy
  • A: Associated Symptoms "Besides the pain, have you noticed swelling, stiffness, weakness, or other issues?"
    • SWELLING
      • Olecranon bursitis
      • Arthritis (RA, Gout)
    • STIFFNESS
      • Arthritis (esp. morning)
    • WEAKNESS
      • Weak grip
      • Nerve palsy
    • NUMBNESS
      • Nerve compression
    • CLICKING / LOCKING
      • Loose body
  • P: Progression "Since it started, has the pain gotten better, worse, or stayed the same?"
  • P: Pattern "Is the pain constant or does it come and go? Is it worse at a particular time?"
    • INTERMITTENT (worse with activity)
      • Epicondylitis
    • CONSTANT
      • Fracture
      • Infection
    • WORSE AT NIGHT
      • Cubital tunnel syndrome
  • E: Effect on Life "How has this elbow pain been affecting your daily activities, work, or hobbies?"
    • Daily activities?
    • Work?
    • Hobbies?
    • Sleep?
  • R: Red Flags "I need to ask a few specific questions to rule out anything serious."
    • HOT, RED JOINT + FEVER
      • Septic Arthritis
    • MAJOR TRAUMA / DEFORMITY
      • Fracture-dislocation
    • SUDDEN "POP" + BRUISING (Popeye Sign)
      • Distal biceps rupture
    • PROGRESSIVE WEAKNESS/NUMBNESS
      • Acute nerve injury
    • HISTORY OF CANCER
      • Metastasis
  • S: Severity / Scale "On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate the pain?"
