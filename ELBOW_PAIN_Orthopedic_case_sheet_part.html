1ST COMPLAINT: ELBOW PAIN

S: Site
  • Dominant arm ?
  • Non-dominant arm ?
  • LATERAL (Outside)
    • Tennis Elbow
    • Radial tunnel syndrome
  • MEDIAL (Inside)
    • Golfer's Elbow
    • Cubital Tunnel Syndrome
  • POSTERIOR (Back)
    • Olecranon bursitis
    • Triceps tendinopathy
  • ANTERIOR (Front)
    • Distal biceps tendinopathy
"Where exactly is the pain in your elbow? Can you point with one finger?"
O: Onset
  • ACUTE / SUDDEN
    • Fracture/Dislocation
    • Tendon/Ligament rupture
  • GRADUAL / CHRONIC
    • Overuse (Epicondylitis)
    • Arthritis
"When did the pain start? Was it from an injury, or did it come on gradually?"
D: Duration
"How long have you had this pain for? Days, weeks, or months?"
A/R: Aggravating & Relieving Factors
  • AGGRAVATING
    • neck movements
      • cervical radiculopathy
      • neck fractures
    • Gripping, lifting, twisting forearm (Epicondylitis)
    • Pressure on the area
  • RELIEVING
    • Rest
    • Ice/Heat
    • Avoiding certain movements
"What makes the pain worse? What makes it better?"
C: Character
  • DULL ACHE
    • Epicondylitis
    • Osteoarthritis
  • BURNING / TINGLING
    • Nerve entrapment
    • Cervical radiculopathy
  • SHARP / STABBING
    • Loose body
    • Acute tear
  • THROBBING
    • Infection
    • Severe bursitis
"What does the pain feel like? Is it a sharp pain, a dull ache, burning, or tingling?"
R: Radiation
  • DOWN FOREARM (Lateral)
    • Tennis Elbow
  • TO 4TH/5TH FINGERS (Medial)
    • Cubital Tunnel Syndrome
  • FROM NECK/SHOULDER
    • Cervical Radiculopathy
"Does the pain travel anywhere from your elbow?"
A: Associated Symptoms
  • SWELLING
    • Olecranon bursitis
    • Arthritis (RA, Gout)
  • STIFFNESS
    • Arthritis (esp. morning)
  • WEAKNESS
    • Weak grip
    • Nerve palsy
  • NUMBNESS
    • Nerve compression
  • CLICKING / LOCKING
    • Loose body
"Besides the pain, have you noticed swelling, stiffness, weakness, or other issues?"
P: Progression
"Since it started, has the pain gotten better, worse, or stayed the same?"
P: Pattern
  • INTERMITTENT (worse with activity)
    • Epicondylitis
  • CONSTANT
    • Fracture
    • Infection
  • WORSE AT NIGHT
    • Cubital tunnel syndrome
"Is the pain constant or does it come and go? Is it worse at a particular time?"
E: Effect on Life
  • Daily activities?
  • Work?
  • Hobbies?
  • Sleep?
"How has this elbow pain been affecting your daily activities, work, or hobbies?"
R: Red Flags
  • HOT, RED JOINT + FEVER
    • Septic Arthritis
  • MAJOR TRAUMA / DEFORMITY
    • Fracture-dislocation
  • SUDDEN "POP" + BRUISING (Popeye Sign)
    • Distal biceps rupture
  • PROGRESSIVE WEAKNESS/NUMBNESS
    • Acute nerve injury
  • HISTORY OF CANCER
    • Metastasis
"I need to ask a few specific questions to rule out anything serious."
S: Severity / Scale
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate the pain?"
